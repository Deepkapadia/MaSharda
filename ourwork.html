Education

We support poor students in region, so no children are deprived of education and he should be able to succeed in life despite of his unfortunate economic condition. We also conduct an exam to provide scholarships. Explore

Farmer support

We are always looking for new ways to support/Educate farmers of the hills region to produce better results. Farmers should be able to sell their products in the market at a fair price. Explore
